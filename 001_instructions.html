Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully.

Procedure

The whole test consits of 35 scenes of artificial indoor environment, which are presented on your screen in the form of interactive panoramatic picture. You can only rotate your field of view (as you would rotate your head) using your mouse. You cannot move through the scenes.

Task

Imagine that there is a fire in the building. You just ran out of your office and you came to a crossroads. Your task is to look around you and choose one of the corridors that makes you feel more safe to leave the building. But be careful, you only have 10 seconds to make your decision.

Scene controlling

You choose corridor by pressing the left mouse button. After selecting the corridor another scene will be loaded automatically. By moving the mouse while pressing the left mouse button you can rotate the scene, which equals to the rotation of head in the real life.


Training

Now four training tasks will follow on which you will be able to try out the interaction with the scenes and the selection of the corridors.
