Please read the following instructions carefully

Procedure

The whole test consists of 35 scenes of an artificial indoor environment, presented on your screen in the form of an interactive panoramic picture. Using your mouse you can only rotate your field of view (as you would rotate your head). You cannot move through the scenes.

Task

Imagine that there is a fire in the building. You just ran out of your office, and you came to a crossroads. Your task is to look around you and choose one of the corridors that makes you feel safer leaving the building. But be careful, you only have 10 seconds to make your decision.

Scene controlling

You choose the corridor by pressing the left mouse button. After selecting the corridor, another scene will be loaded automatically. By moving the mouse you can rotate the scene to the sides, which equals the rotation of the head in the real life.


Training

Now four training tasks will follow on which you will be able to try out the interaction with the scenes and the selection of the corridors. Please work in full-screen mode (simply use the F11 key to turn it on). Please use a mouse (not a touchpad) for interaction with the scenes.
