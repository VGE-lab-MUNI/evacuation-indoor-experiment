Welcome to our interactive indoor evacuation online test

Introduction

This test was developed in cooperation between two Departments of Geography at Brno Masaryk University and Ghent University.

The main goal of this test is to gain insight into human decision making when choosing an evacuation route during stress conditions. For this purpose virtual tours will be used, which will display a spheric picture of a building model. The test consists of a questionnaire with your basic personal characteristics, a training of interaction with the scenes, the experimental scenes and a follow-up questionnaire. The whole test should not take longer than 10 minutes. There are no risks in taking the test.

Requirements

This online test requires that your device supports WebGL. You can test it here or here. If possible, work in full screen mode (you can use the F11 key to turn it on).

Data collection

In this test we collect basic pesronal characteristics and subjective opinions on the displayed scenes. Please be also aware, that during the test, data on your interaction (mouse movement, keystrokes) will be collected. All data is collected anonymously and there are no attempts to store your IP address. Collected data will only be used for the purpose of evaluating the experiment and subsequent publications.
