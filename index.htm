Welcome to our interactive indoor evacuation online test


This test was developed in cooperation between the Department of Geography at Brno Masaryk University and the Department of Geography at Ghent University.

The main goal of this test is to gain insight into human decision-making when choosing an evacuation route during stress conditions. For this purpose the virtual tours will be used, which will display a spheric picture of a building model. The test consists of questionnaire with your basic personal characteristics, training of interaction with the scenes, the experimental scenes and a follow-up questionnaire. Whole test should not take longer than 10 minutes. There are no risks in taking the test.

This online test requires that your device supports WebGL. You can test it here or here.

If possible, work in full screen mode. For example, use the F11 key to turn it on.

Please be aware, that during the test, data on your interaction (mouse movement, keystrokes) will be collected. The data is collected anonymously and will only be used for the purpose of evaluating the experiment and subsequent publications.

By proceeding further to the test, you are consenting, that you are taking the test voluntarily and you are aware, that you can withdraw from the test anytime. If you want to participate, please use the button below to enter the test.
